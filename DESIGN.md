# Kurage Design Rules

## White Studio Rule For VTuber Videos

Kurage VTuber mode must not use black or dark-mode backgrounds. This applies to HyperFrames templates, title overlays, subtitle cards, avatar cards, generated screenshots, YouTube upload assets, and public demo videos.

Preferred direction:

- white / off-white / pale aqua canvas
- light coastal gradients
- white translucent subtitle cards with dark ink text
- subtle blue borders and soft shadows
- bright, commercial, trustworthy impression for business owners

Forbidden:

- `background: #000`
- black full-screen title overlays
- black translucent caption boxes
- dark navy or cyberpunk gradients as the primary screen
- horror-like or developer-tool dark mode for public Kurage demos

If a video needs contrast, add light cards, white veils, or soft blue shadows. Do not solve readability by putting text on a black box.
